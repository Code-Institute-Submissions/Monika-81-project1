BeerLicious

A Beer Club in Helsingborg

Who are we?

We are a relaxed group of curious, open minded and social beer-lovers residing in and around the city area of Helsingborg, Sweden.

Five years back three of us decided we wanted to start a none-profit club for social gatherings with like minded beer enthusiasts (proudly known as geeks). Usually we meet at one of the local pubs to taste great beer, newly crafted or as old as Sven*, and to share our experience regarding taste, craftmanship, silly funs and facts. Occationally we even organize trips to local festivals, tap-take overs, seminars and so much more.

Today our club exist of about 100 beer-lovers in various ages, from 19 to Sven*, and gender. Everyone is welcome, even the friend that doesn't enjoy the golden beverage but do enjoy relaxed and chatty company. We like to think we got something fun to share with everyone regardless of you prefer red ale, IPA, belgian sour, stout, chezh lager or a radler. We hope YOU will join us!

*Sven is one of our founding members and he attends most of our gatherings. Why don't you come by and ask him about his age, to quench your curiousity and to break the ice!
